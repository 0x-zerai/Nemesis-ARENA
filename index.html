Frequently Asked — Nemesis

What is Nemesis?

Nemesis is a DeFi trading protocol built for Ethereum and EVM-compatible chains that enables users to buy (go long) or short any on-chain token via its liquidity framework, without relying on derivatives. It offers embedded leverage of up to ~5×, fully on-chain trading, market making, and liquidations.

What are Nemesis Arena Tickets?

Nemesis Arena Tickets are an NFT collection (limited edition) tied to the Nemesis ecosystem, granting holders exclusive access or allocation rights—such as participation in the upcoming $NEMESIS public token sale.

What is $NEMESIS and its public sale?

$NEMESIS is the utility/governance token of the Nemesis protocol. The public sale is planned with allocation reserved for certain ticket-holders (e.g., those holding Arena Tickets) and a capped funding round.
